Company Story and History

Our Company's founding

Coltivare was founded in 25 A.C (After Calamity) by John and Martha Coltive. Originally farmers themselves, they desired an easier way to get their products from their blackberry farm in the mountains down into the valley town's marketplace. To do so, they collaborated with the Trog engineer Arith to create the first delivery dirigible. Eventually, they found it was not only more profitable to create farming equipment for others, but more exciting as well. After selling their blackberry farm and forming a long term pact with Arith, they founded Coltivare farminng equipment.
